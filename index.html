Rules:

  • Start: Players take turns placing X or O in any open cell of the overall grid.
  • Local Board: Move is dictated by opponent's last move, directing to a local board. Win local boards to influence opponent's next move.
  • Winning: Win local boards by getting three symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Forced Move: If a player's opponent directs them to a local board that has already been won, they can choose to make their move in any open cell of any legal local board.
  • Strategy: Aim to win local boards. Forced move allowed if directed to a won board.
  • Victory: Win by getting three local boards in a row.
  • Draw: Draw if all boards are filled without a winner.
